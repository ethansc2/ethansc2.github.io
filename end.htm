Efficiency of different automotive fuel types.


Electricity is by far the most efficient fuel type.
Electric cars average highway MPG is 181% greater than that of gasoline and 112% greater than that of diesel.
Electric cars average city MPG is 510% greater than that of gasoline and 387% greater than that of diesel.

The difference in mean between diesel and gasoline under the 5% significance level is only significant for city MPG,
while the difference for highway MPG is not significant. Due to the small sample size of diesel cars, this could indicate
that there may not be a large difference between the true means for diesel and gasoline cars.
